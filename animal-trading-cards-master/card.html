Snow Leopard

In Nepal, the main prey of snow leopards are Blue Sheep, and they are more closely related to tigers than they are leopards.

  • Sientific Name: Panthera uncia
  • Average Length: 1.1m
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Average Weight: 32kg (adult)

Snow leopard lives in moutain rages of Central and South Asia, listed as vulnerable. They have gray fur with black spots on head and have large pattern of the tail. They have small ears to minimize heat loss and they have a thinck layer of fur to keep them warm in cold weather.
